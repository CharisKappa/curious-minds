Things I'm
figuring out.

A shelf of long-form tutorials I write to teach myself things properly — and then leave here, in plain language, in case they help you too.

Bilingual · English & Greek · Built slowly, on purpose

I learn a subject best when I have to explain it from the ground up — no jargon I can hide behind, no steps quietly skipped. So that's what these are: each tutorial is a topic I wanted to genuinely understand, written out the long way, the way I wish someone had written it for me. They assume nothing. They build up. They have opinions.

Everything here is bilingual, because the people I most want to read these think in Greek. Pick a tutorial and start anywhere — though, like most things worth learning, they reward being read in order.
