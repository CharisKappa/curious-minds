What this tutorial covers

Energy is not a side story in human history — it is human history. Every empire, every war, every economic revolution, every cultural shift can ultimately be traced to a single question: where does the energy come from, and who controls it?

This tutorial traces that question from the first deliberate fire in prehistoric Africa to the solar panels going up on rooftops right now. Along the way, we meet the people who controlled energy, the wars fought over it, the economics it created, and the crisis it has brought us to.
