Behaviour, Evolution
& the Mind

From primate origins to AI-powered manipulation: the complete story of the human brain, what evolution built into it, and how that architecture has been exploited — from the first campfire to the smartphone.

8 chapters · ~60 min · EN · ΕΛ

This tutorial asks the question that sits underneath all the others: what kind of animal are we? Understanding energy, money, technology, and politics is easier once you understand the primate brain running the show — its tribal loyalties, its status anxieties, its deference to authority, and its extraordinary vulnerability to anyone who takes the time to understand how it works.
