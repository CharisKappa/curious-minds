# Tech, Rewinding the Tape — Tutorial 02

A history of technology read through three lenses — what each invention did to **money**, what it did to **power**, and what it did to **daily life** — from the first stone tool to today's AI models. Eight chapters walk the timeline in order and ask the same three questions of every era.

**Status:** In progress · shells scaffolded (EN + EL), content being drafted. Each chapter page exists with the full house structure (header nav, TOC sidebar, chapter-end navigation, footer) and a "content coming soon" placeholder; the prose is written chapter by chapter. The Greek `-el.html` twins are in place and awaiting translation once the English lands.

## Chapters

1. **Origins** — 3M BC → 0 AD
2. **The Long Middle** — 0 → 1500
3. **First Acceleration** — 1500 → 1800
4. **The Industrial Era** — 1800 → 1900
5. **The Electric & Atomic Century** — 1900 → 1970
6. **The Digital Era** — 1970 → 2010
7. **The AI Era** — 2010 → today
8. **Patterns & Implications** — across all eras
