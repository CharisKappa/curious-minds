# Money, Decoded — Tutorial 01

A field guide to money, the economy, and investing — from the cotton paper in your pocket to the global financial system it represents, and how to put your share of it to work instead of watching it quietly disappear. Four chapters (History → Economy → Investments → Practice & the traps), each building on the last, plus three interactive calculators (inflation erosion, compounding, fee drag).

**Status:** Complete · EN + EL · ~60,000 words · Edition One, May 2026.
